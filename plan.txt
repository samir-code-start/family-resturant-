PROJECT OVERVIEW

Restaurant Name: [Asha family Restaurant]
Cuisine Type: Authentic Indian Cuisine
Project Goal: Create a professional, user-friendly restaurant website to showcase menu, attract customers, and facilitate online orders/reservations

Target Audience:

- Food enthusiasts exploring authentic Indian flavors
- Tourists and travelers looking for dining options
- Corporate clients for catering services
- Families and groups for celebrations


WEBSITE OBJECTIVES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Primary Objectives:
1. Showcase authentic Indian cuisine with high-quality visuals
2. Provide easy access to complete menu with prices
3. Enable online reservations and table booking
4. Facilitate online food ordering (takeout/delivery)
5. Build brand recognition and trust
6. Increase customer engagement through social media integration
7. Improve search engine visibility (SEO)

Secondary Objectives:
1. Showcase customer testimonials and reviews
2. Highlight special events and promotions
3. Provide location and contact information
4. Share restaurant story and chef's profile
5. Display hygiene and quality certifications

DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Design Style: Modern, Clean, Culturally Authentic

Color Palette:
- Primary: Warm saffron/orange tones (representing Indian spices)
- Secondary: Deep burgundy/maroon (traditional Indian colors)
- Accent: Gold/brass tones (luxury and authenticity)
- Neutral: Cream, beige backgrounds
- Text: Dark brown/charcoal for readability

Typography:
- Headings: Elegant serif or display font (culturally appropriate)
- Body: Clean, readable sans-serif
- Menu items: Clear, easy-to-read font

Visual Elements:
- High-quality food photography
- Traditional Indian patterns as subtle backgrounds
- Spice and ingredient imagery
- Authentic restaurant ambiance photos
- Cultural motifs and decorative elements
